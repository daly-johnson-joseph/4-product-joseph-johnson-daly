About Us


Tradition since 2019

Kylie, Hayilice, and Samantha started off in the small state of Massachusetts. Their goal was to bring the special taste of the food held in their restaurant comes from all the different places located around the world all the way from France to Thailand. It all started when they went on vacation to Honolulu, Hawaii. All they wanted was food from home and came to the realization that everyone wants a piece of wherever they went. The food we carry in Johnson, Joseph Daly Dishes consists of homemade recipes from generations before us. We recognized that having a piece of home travel with you is a very important part of a vacation so every item on our menu costs less than twenty dollars which goes toward funding special causes such as saving the marine life and coral reefs

Entrées

Poke Bowls

Poke is made from our local fish served either as an appetizer or as a main course and is always one of our customer’s favorites.

The Classic Pasta Dish

This classic Italian dish uses fresh and tasty sun-riped tomatoes, with a little bit of garlic and fresh basil leaves. With a delicious combination of marinara sauce and penne pasta, this dish is always a winner.

The Hamburger

Our freshly baked buns combined with rich vegetables from our local farm, served with your choice of meat to make a delicious USA hamburger.

Falafel

This Middle Eastern classic meal is our deep-fried doughnut-shaped patty made from ground chickpeas, fava beans, or both, your choice! We add herbs, spices, and onion to the dough to add a splash of flavor.

Pot-au-feu

Pot-au-feu is a French beef stew. According to chef Raymond Blanc, pot-au-feu is "the quintessence of French family cuisine.” The dish has many variations; although, the one made by us is always enjoyed by our customers.

Dumplings

This original Chinese meal consists of a broad classification for a dish that consists of pieces of dough wrapped around our delicious filling. Choices for the dough are our famous bread, flour or potatoes. There is the option to be filled with meat, fish, cheese, vegetables, fruits or sweets.

Goulash

This Hungary plate consists of soup with meat and vegetables, seasoned with paprika and signature spices.

Chilli Crab

Chilli crab is a delightful Singaporean seafood dish. We make this dish with mud crabs which are stir-fried in a semi-thick, sweet and savory tomato-and-chili-based sauce.

Dessert

Lilikoʻi bars

Hawaii’s version of everyone’s favorite, lemon bars. Our buttery crust is layered with a sweet and tart lilikoʻi filling, otherwise known as passion fruit.

Boston Cream Pie

This Boston Cream Pie is a classic cake – not a pie at all! It’s a lovely vanilla cake layered with fresh pastry cream and chocolate ganache – all from scratch!

Apple Strudel

With a mix of apples and cinnamon, you'll be cutting yourself slice after slice without even thinking twice! This German recipe is especially popular in the fall, right when apple season starts to pick up steam.

What else?

Crème brûlée

A French classic is a delicious dessert that contains a rich custard tart topped with a texturally contrasting layer of hardened caramelized sugar.

Baklava

In Turkey, this desert is a popular dessert throughout the year. Baklava is a rich, sweet dessert pastry made of layers of filo filled with chopped nuts and sweetened and held together with syrup or honey.

Tres Leches

Tres Leches is a classic Spanish cake that consists of a sponge cake soaked in milk. With no butter added to this recipe, the Tres Leches cake is very light with many air bubbles.

Cannoli

Cannolis are Italian pastries that originated on the island of Sicily. Cannolis consist of tube-shaped shells of fried pastry dough, filled with a sweet, creamy filling.

Mochi

Mochi is a Japanese rice cake made of mochigome.This special rice is pounded into paste and molded into our delicious pastry shapes.
